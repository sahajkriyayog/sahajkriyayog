🙏 The Path of Kriya Yoga

The Three Pillars of Practice

According to Patanjali, Kriya Yoga consists of three essential components simplified by Lahiri Mahasaya for modern practitioners.

🔥

Tapasya (Austerity)

Physical discipline, such as dietary control, minimal sleep, and yoga postures, designed to purify the body and calm the senses.

📖

Svadhyaya (Self-study)

Verbal austerity, studying scriptures, speaking truthfully, and avoiding harmful or unnecessary talk to bring focus inward.

🙏

Ishvara Pranidhana (Devotion)

Mental surrender and dedicating the fruits of all actions to God, leading to inner peace and divine connection.
