About the Ashram

The Brahmarshi Satyananda Sannyas Ashram exists to spread the profound science of Kriya Yoga to the general public. Through the Gita Prachar Mandali, we have published the spiritual booklet "Kriya Yoga and the Guru Lineage" to introduce seekers to this transformative path.

Our ashram serves as a haven for spiritual practitioners, offering authentic Kriya Yoga initiation, guided meditation sessions, and immersion in the sacred teachings of the guru lineage. We welcome seekers from all walks of life.
